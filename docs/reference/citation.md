> Kazem S, Kumar K, Yang J, Benitiere F, Huguet G, Mollon J, et al. **Determinants of functional burden pleiotropy and gene dosage responses across human traits.** medRxiv. Preprint version 3. https://www.medrxiv.org/content/10.1101/2025.02.25.25322833v3
